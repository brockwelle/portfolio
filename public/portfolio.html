Cadillac Super Cruise Map Viewer

I co-developed the original pilot for the map viewer using Minnesota Map Server, ESRI service backgrounds, and shapefiles. I helped bootstrap a team of great developers in Limerick, Ireland, to build it out and harden it for production. They worked out how to conform to Cadillac's strict Style Guide and took it much further.

Map with Programme Gannt

I developed Python customizations to ESRI ArcGIS which consumed data from integrated Primavera P6 project managment databases to produce PDFs of map output depicting multiple transportation project timelines and integration locations in preparation for 2022 FIFA World Cup.

The Other Kind of Architecture

In a previous life [as in ... before AutoCAD] I worked for the Architect who designed this library in Fort Bend County, TX. I hand-rendered several of the elevations from the architect's floor plans and applied text via hand-drafted lettering and Keuffel & Esser's Leroy ink lettering system for most of the sheets.
